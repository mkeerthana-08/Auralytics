Engine Whisperer

Sound-Based Fault Diagnosis

An intelligent acoustic diagnostic tool capable of identifying mechanical health patterns from a simple 5-second audio record of a running engine.

⚖️ The Knowledge Gap

Experienced industrial mechanics hold an intuitive master skill: they can diagnose a damaged bearing, an unbalanced shaft, or a misfiring engine cylinder simply by leaning close and listening. Junior technicians and everyday operators don't possess this deep acoustic pattern library.

💵 Democratizing Expertise

This project captures that exact master-level audio intuition and packs it tightly into an accessible, $0-cost digital system. It changes localized diagnostic knowledge into software scalable for anybody with a microphone.

📈 Industrial Value Add

By capturing physical signatures right when machines cross the line from healthy to failing, this system bridges raw hardware realities with predictive systems like CAT ADSD or TIS performance bulletins.

"An experienced service technician hears an anomaly and knows exactly where to look. I built a system to duplicate that intelligence instantly."
🎯 Core Purpose

Engine Whisperer listens to running machinery, isolates complex frequencies, checks them against structural sound classifications, and warns operators about internal system wear before sudden operational failure occurs.

The framework transforms acoustic mechanical noise into structured labels over 4 core architectural movements. Click any phase to expand engineering breakdowns:

01
Acoustic Signal Capturing
Captures 5 full seconds of unfiltered engine operational vibrations through native microphone arrays. Saves raw arrays into clean, standard wave structures ready for matrix computation.
▶
02
MFCC Feature Extraction
Leverages the Librosa library to isolate Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCCs). This process strips background workspace echo out and isolates specific harmonic sound timelines unique to engine components.
▶
03
Pattern Matching & Classification
Feeds target feature matrices straight into Scikit-learn trained models. The data structures are mapped directly against structural labels: Healthy, Worn, or Impending Failure.
▶
04
Automated Inspection Reports
Translates analytical output vectors into human-readable field summaries. Automatically specifies component health flags, pinpointing precisely which mechanical parts need service attention.
